Hannah Tomes

University of Leeds Graduate

Hi! I'm Hannah, a 22-year-old English and History of Art graduate from Leeds Uni, avid reader, gin enthusiast, and budding journalist. Taking a year out to travel from August, starting on the MA Print Journalism course at Sheffield Uni September 2017.

Words for the Guardian, The Telegraph, The Huffington Post, The Tab, Leeds Living Magazine, and Savage Thrills. Ex Arts Editor at The Gryphon newspaper, interned at The York Press and The Shropshire Star.
